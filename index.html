Our Services

We professionally install head units that look factory-fitted, deliver enhanced control, connectivity, and audio quality. Experience the full depth of your music with high-performance speakers, subwoofers, and amps that transform your vehicle into a concert hall.

Protect what matters. From advanced alarm systems to GPS tracking and immobilisers, we install smart security solutions that give you confidence, whether you're parked at home or on the move. Get real-time alerts, remote access, and peace of mind knowing your car is protected.

From accident recording to safer parking, improved awareness, and reversing with confidence in tight spaces. Whether you're navigating busy streets, backing into a garage, or leaving your car unattended, our camera solutions give you an extra layer of protection.

Our parking sensor systems help you detect obstacles with pinpoint accuracy, reducing the risk of scrapes, dents, and costly repairs. Whether you're reversing in tight spaces, navigating tricky driveways, or squeezing into parallel parks, parking sensors give you feedback when it matters most.

Adventure-ready, the right way. We install 4WD accessories — from lights to dual battery setups to make sure your rig is equipped for any terrain, with durability and functionality at the core. Built for performance. Wired to last. Ready when it counts.

We specialise in custom automotive work designed to match your vehicle and your vision. Whether you're chasing a clean, factory-style upgrade or a fully tailored interior or exterior build, we approach every job with attention to detail and a focus on quality.
